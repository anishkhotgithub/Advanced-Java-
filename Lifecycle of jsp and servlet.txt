CGI VS Servlet:

	CGI (Common Gateway Interface)	
	Servlet


Lifecyle Of a servlet:

	1) Convert servlet to bytecode.   
	2) Load servlet instance.
	3) Call init() method.
	4) Call service() method.
	5) Call destroy() method.

Lifecyle of a jsp:		

	1) Translate jsp to servlet.
	2) Convert to bytecode.
	3) Create instance.
	4) Load Servlet instance.
	5) Call jspInit() method.
	6) Call _jspService() Method.
	7) Call jspDestory() method.
